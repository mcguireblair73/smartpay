What is Automated Payments?

Automated Payments allows you to link a bank account to a location.

Automated Payments sums up your net transactions for a location and transfers it to a bank account.

Automated Payments runs the 1st of the month at 3AM PST/PDT time.

Automated Payments has an additional fee of $5/month/location.

Automated Payments saves you time and money.
